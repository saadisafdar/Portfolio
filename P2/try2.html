Who
I Am

I'm Saadi Safdar — a Computer Science student, developer, and creator based in Wah, Pakistan. I build things that matter: desktop tools, AI-powered systems, web apps, and everything in between.

My technical scope covers Python (automation, OpenCV computer vision, GUI apps), Java (desktop & system-level software), C++ (algorithms, data structures, low-level logic), and the modern web stack with JavaScript, React, and Node.js.

I'm particularly drawn to problems at the intersection of AI and real-world utility — whether that's a helmet-detection safety system, an intelligent route engine, or a career guidance platform. Clean code and continuous improvement are non-negotiable for me.

Outside of development, I run SynzaFeed — a content brand across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and more — which keeps my creative and communication skills sharp. I believe a developer who can explain ideas clearly is twice as effective.
